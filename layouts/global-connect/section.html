Premise

What if we could directly tap the student community? What if we could give them a place to ask mentors for advice, collaborate with peers, and stay informed with the ever growing technologies of today? What if students expanded their knowledge and applied their skills beyond the typical classroom setting?

Manifesto

diesmo5 is committed to building a global community through a free chat server that brings diverse groups of people together. diesmo5 will distribute monthly newsletters that will showcase relevant articles, free resources, upcoming events, and job opportunities. diesmo5 will support non-profits & school organizations in helping students develop skills in the field of IT.

Our Chat Server

diesmo5 has designed a Discord Server that allows students and mentors ask each other questions, get help on projects, network with their peers, and share their journey in a safe place filled with like minded people. Join our server and meet new people.

Our Newsletter

diesmo5 has an entire team dedicated in compiling & creating the most value-driven newsletter. The newsletter is intended to highlight new technologies & resources that are available to help students and professionals keep up with the continuously changing tech world. It will also promote local events and job opportunities.

Our Courses

diesmo5 has partnered with local tech companies & engineers to compile free digital courses students can use to enhance their skills in software and hardware development. The purpose of these courses is not to re-invent the wheel, rather to get rid of all the noise. Thousands of pieces of educational content already exist, our job is to compile the ones that bring the most value and sequence it in a purposeful way in which our so that our students will be empowered to build projects of their own.

Our Support

diesmo5 offers support when it comes to events, hackathons, workshops, and student projects in the form of providing free software, funding, and mentors. Reach out to us to collaborate on your next project.

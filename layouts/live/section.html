Today's Workshop: A Practical Guide to Becoming a Web Developer

What to Expect: Today's workshop can be broken down into two parts: a presentation and a hands on project. we will be going over the different tools, langauges, frameworks involved in web development. We will also cover how those tools work and relate to one another. During the presentation, we will discuss the roadmap towards becoming a Front-End Web Developer, Back-End Web Developer, and/or a Full-Stack Developer. For the second half of the workshop, we will go through the process of building a responsive website using modern design frameworks, the same tools used to build your favorite apps/websites like Twitter, Facebook, Instagram.
Desired Outcomes: Every attendee should leave with a strong understanding of web development and have a road map that they can use to dive deeper into their career path. They should also be going home with their very own website, where they can freely choose to design this website as a portfolio site for themselves, a club they are in, or to showcase something they are passionate about.
